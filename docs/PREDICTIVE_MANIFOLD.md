# Predictive Manifold

## 1. Introduction

The predictive manifold is the forward-looking engine of Vantage 2.0. It generates trajectory families based on the interaction of Fuel (F), Entropy (S), and Maturity (M), incorporating regime boundaries and drift dynamics. This document defines the structure of the manifold, the rules governing curvature, and the conditions under which trajectories diverge, converge, or undergo phase transitions.
